He walks over to the chicken coop to say goodbye. He takes some extra food and scatters it with the chickens. 
When he has finished spreading the food he turns around but as he turns around he sees something shiny between the chickens. 
Norr lifts the chicken and sees a golden egg there. He is amazed and delighted to have never seen a golden egg. 
but the happiness and joy is quickly suppressed by the tension and fear of traveling alone. He puts the golden egg in his coat and he leaves for the village
