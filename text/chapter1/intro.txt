This story begins with a family of 3 that runs a small farm next to the "mountain of treasures".
The family contains of a mother, a father and a aboy. Every on of them got there own tasks on the farm.
The mother works with the cows and makes sure there is enough milk produced.
The father cuts down the grain in the fields and bakes bread.
And the boy takes care of the chicken.

The family reserves 2 stashes of products they gain from the farm.
The first stash is for the local people. Every Saturday they go to the nearby village and sell there farm products on the market.
The second stash is for the king. Every first Monday of the month the family has to deliver 2kgs of grain, 10 liter milk, 12 bread & 2 chickens to the king. to pay off their mortgage.

Only this time the father is sick and the boy (you) is forced to deliver the package to the king alone.
Trough the mountains of treasures you will you experience things you have never seen before.
Are you worthy to deliver the package!?
