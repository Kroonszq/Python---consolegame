After a while walking on fland land Norr enters the mountain from here on the journey starts official.
The slope is not so steep yet this is the beginning of the mountain. Together with his horse, they slowly ride up. 
Norr looks around and sees trees several meters high and hears the birds chirping. 
The tension and anxiety subsides a little bit, maybe it's not so scary after all, Norr thinks. 
After some time, Norr arrives at the village. His father had asked him to pay his good friends taxes and take them with him.
 Norr is thirsty from the long travel. He doubts whether he will have a drink first or pick up the package right away
